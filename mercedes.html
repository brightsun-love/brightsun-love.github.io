Mercedes-Benz E200

The Mercedes-Benz E200 is a luxury sedan focused on comfort, advanced technology, and premium experience. It is designed more for relaxation than aggressive driving.

Overview

Engine: 2.0L turbo petrol
Power: ~201 bhp
Torque: 320 Nm
Gearbox: 9-speed automatic
Mileage: ~15 km/l
Price: ₹78 lakh – ₹1 crore

Key Highlights

• Best-in-class rear seat comfort
• Advanced digital dashboard
• Premium interior materials
• Smooth and refined driving experience

Gallery

Final Verdict

Perfect for buyers who want luxury, comfort, and brand value. Not ideal for those seeking sporty performance.
