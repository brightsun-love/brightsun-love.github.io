Maruti Dzire XZI+ Review (2026)

The Maruti Dzire XZI+ is one of India’s most popular compact sedans, known for its fuel efficiency, practicality, and low maintenance cost. It is designed for everyday usability rather than performance.

🔍 Quick Specs

Engine1.2L Petrol
Power~89 bhp
Mileage~22 km/l
TransmissionManual / AMT
Price₹8–10 lakh

✨ Key Features

  • Touchscreen infotainment system
  • Apple CarPlay & Android Auto
  • Cruise control
  • Automatic climate control
  • Push-button start

👍 Pros

  • Excellent fuel efficiency
  • Low maintenance cost
  • Easy to drive in city
  • Good resale value

👎 Cons

  • Not powerful
  • Build quality could be better
  • Not ideal for high-speed driving

🏁 Final Verdict

The Dzire XZI+ is perfect for buyers who want a reliable, economical, and comfortable car for daily use. It is not meant for performance enthusiasts but excels in practicality.
