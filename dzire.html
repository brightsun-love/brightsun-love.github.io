Maruti Dzire XZI+ Review (2026)

The Maruti Dzire XZI+ is one of India’s most practical and reliable compact sedans. Built for everyday driving, it focuses on efficiency, comfort, and low ownership cost rather than performance or luxury.

Quick Specifications

Engine1.2L Petrol
Power~89 bhp
Mileage~22 km/l
TransmissionManual / AMT
Price₹8–10 lakh

Key Features

  • Touchscreen infotainment system
  • Apple CarPlay & Android Auto
  • Cruise control
  • Automatic climate control
  • Push-button start

Pros

  • Excellent fuel efficiency
  • Low maintenance cost
  • Easy to drive in city traffic
  • Strong resale value

Cons

  • Not performance-oriented
  • Average build quality
  • Limited highway excitement

Final Verdict

The Dzire XZI+ is ideal for buyers who want a dependable, economical, and comfortable car for daily use. It delivers where it matters most—efficiency and practicality.
